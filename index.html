About me

Hi and welcome to my responsive Bootstrap website. My name is Gabe Scoggin and I'm a full stack developer and project manager. I have experience with building a bunch of different websites, but I'm always looking for my next project. I'm proficient in HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. For the last 10 years I've been working at Google as an Engieering Program Manager in Mountain View. I've worked on large scale GIS Mapping Projects for Google Maps. I've built operations teams in Hyderabad IN, Dublin IE, Seattle WA, and Mountain View CA. I've designed mapping tools and processes to build sub-meter accurate vector maps at scale. I've developed strategic partnerhips with data providers, large and small. Most recently I've been working in Ad Policy and User Data Protection at Google. I enable Engineers and Operators to stop bad actors on the web and keep Google Ads safe for our users.

About me personally, I grew up in San Francisco, and it's my favorite place on the planet. I'm married to my beautiful and caring wife, Kara Scoggin, who is a marketing director at Coursera. We have a 6 month old baby boy named Maverick. He's a nut, but we love him to death. We also have a crazy black lab mix named Midnight. We like to spend our time in Tahoe in the winter and Napa in the summer. I love sports and I like to travel. Skiing is my favorite hobby, I also love food & wine, art, and movies. If you want my list of my favorite wineries just ping me on slack.
